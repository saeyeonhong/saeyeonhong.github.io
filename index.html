About

I am a third-semester M.S. student in AI Convergence at Ewha Womans University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Se-Eun Oh. Prior to this, I received my B.S. from Ewha womans University in 2024. My research is following work of DeepCoFFEA.

My research interests include:

  • Network security & privacy
    Tor traffic analysis, anonymity systems, flow correlation attacks using ANN-based methods (HNSW, CAGRA)
  • Privacy attacks on generative models
    Membership inference on TTS systems (2nd author, 1+ year collaboration)
  • Differential privacy
    Foundational theory and applications to secure ML
