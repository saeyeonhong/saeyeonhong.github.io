Projects

ANN-aware metric learning for Tor flow correlation

• In progress · targeting ACSAC 2026

With [Advisor Name] and collaborators.

Extending our CISC-S work on ANN-based flow correlation (HNSW, CAGRA) toward a full system that jointly optimizes the embedding space and the nearest-neighbor index. The goal is to make correlation attacks on Tor scale to realistic-sized traffic corpora while preserving attack accuracy. Builds on the DeepCoFFEA line of work.

Membership inference attacks on text-to-speech models

• In preparation

With [First Author] and [Advisor Name]. Second-author project.

Investigating whether modern TTS systems leak information about their training data through membership inference. Of interest both as a privacy concern and as a probe for understanding memorization in generative audio models.

ANN literature review for network-security applications

• Ongoing

Surveying ANN methods from top-tier database and systems venues (VLDB, ICDE, SIGMOD) and mapping them to the specific demands of traffic-correlation workloads — high-dimensional, partially ordered, adversarially robust. A reading list and notes are maintained on my blog.
