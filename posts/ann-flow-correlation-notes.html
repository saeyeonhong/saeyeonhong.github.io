Hypothesis

  1. Graph-based indices implicitly assume a locally Euclidean manifold.
  2. Flow embeddings, trained with a contrastive loss, may violate this near the boundaries between classes.
  3. Therefore: the choice of index interacts with the choice of loss function — which is the motivation for ANN-aware metric learning.
